Mastermind

Enjoy a game of mastermind.
Click on the color you desire. And now,
click on the bottom field of the gameboard to set color.
Once you set the fourth color, the field is investegated.
white for a color in right place.
black for a color in the wrong place.
If you are a winner, an alert is it saying so.
But if you fail, a new chance is emerging.
Good Luck.
